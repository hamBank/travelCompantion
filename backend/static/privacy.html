Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 June 2026  ·  tripplan.hups.club

Travel Companion is a private, self-hosted trip-planning tool. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Who operates this service

This instance of Travel Companion is operated privately by the site owner for personal use and by explicitly invited users. It is not a public commercial service.

2. Data we collect

  • Google account information — when you sign in with Google we receive your name, email address, and profile picture URL from Google's OAuth service. We store only the email address to verify you are an authorised user; we do not store your password.
  • Trip and itinerary data — any trips, stops, and itinerary items you create or import are stored in the server's SQLite database. This data is yours.
  • Session token — after sign-in, a JSON Web Token (JWT) is stored in your browser's localStorage. It is used to authenticate subsequent requests and expires after 30 days by default.
  • Server logs — the web server (Apache) writes standard access and error logs containing your IP address, request path, and timestamp. Logs are rotated and not shared.

3. How we use your data

  • To authenticate you and restrict access to authorised users only.
  • To store and retrieve your trip itineraries.
  • To diagnose server errors via log files.

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties.

4. Google OAuth

Sign-in is handled by Google's OAuth 2.0 service. When you click "Continue with Google", you are redirected to Google's authentication pages, which are governed by Google's Privacy Policy . We receive only an ID token from which we extract your name, email, and picture URL; we do not receive your Google password or access to any Google services beyond this.

5. Google Sheets import

If the Google Sheets import feature is configured, the server reads data from a specific spreadsheet using a service-account credential stored on the server. No spreadsheet data is shared with third parties.

6. Cookies and local storage

This app does not use cookies. A single JWT token is stored in localStorage to maintain your session. You can clear it by signing out or clearing your browser's site data.

7. Data retention

Your itinerary data is retained indefinitely on the server until you or the operator deletes it. Server access logs are retained for up to 30 days.

8. Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. The server runs on a hardened systemd service with a restricted system user. JWT secrets are stored in an environment file readable only by the application user.

9. Your rights

You may request deletion of your account data by contacting the operator directly. Because this is a private service, formal regulatory data requests should also be directed to the operator.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
